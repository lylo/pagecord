Premium subscribers can run two blogs from the same Pagecord account. This is useful if you want separate spaces for different projects, audiences, or writing styles.

## Opening Manage blogs

1. Log in to your [dashboard](https://pagecord.com/app)
2. Click your blog avatar in the top-right corner
3. Click **Manage blogs**

From there, you can see every blog attached to your account, create a new one, or delete a blog you no longer need.

## Creating a new blog

1. Open **Manage blogs**
2. Click **New blog**
3. Choose a subdomain
4. Add an optional title
5. Click **Create blog**

After creating a blog, Pagecord switches you to the new blog automatically.

## Switching between blogs

Click your blog avatar in the top-right corner, then choose the blog you want to work on.

Each blog has its own posts, pages, settings, custom domain, navigation, subscribers, and analytics.

## Deleting a blog

Open **Manage blogs**, then click **Delete** next to the blog you want to remove.

Deleting a blog cannot be undone. You must always keep at least one blog on your account.

## Blog limits

Free accounts can have one blog. Premium subscribers can have two blogs.

If you are on the free plan, upgrade to Premium from **Settings** → **Billing** to add a second blog.

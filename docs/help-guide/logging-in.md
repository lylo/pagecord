There are two ways to log in to Pagecord: magic email links and passwords.

Magic links are the default. They let you log in via email without having to remember a password. You can add a password to your account if you prefer a traditional login flow, or use Pagecord as a PWA (Progressive Web App) on your phone or desktop.

## Logging in with a magic link

1. Go to [pagecord.com/login](https://pagecord.com/login)
2. Enter your blog subdomain and the email address you registered with
3. Click **Log in**
4. Check your email for a login link (it should only take a few seconds)
5. Click the link to access your dashboard

Magic links can only be used once and they expire after 24 hours.

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder and make sure you entered the same email address you used to create your account. This is the most common mishap.

## Logging in with a password

If you have set a password, choose **Log in with password instead** on the login screen.

Enter your blog subdomain and password, then click **Log in**.

## Setting a password

Pagecord doesn't require you to set a password, but you can set one at any time.

1. Go to [pagecord.com/password_resets/new](https://pagecord.com/password_resets/new)
2. Enter your blog subdomain and the email address you registered your account with
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link and choose a new password

You can also find this from **Settings** → **My Account** when you are logged in.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters.

## Forgotten passwords

If you forget your password, use the same password reset flow to choose a new one.

You can still use magic links to log in, unless you no longer have access to the email address on your account.

## Need help?

If you're struggling to get into your account, contact [support](support.md) from the email address attached to your Pagecord account.

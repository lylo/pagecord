Manage your Pagecord subscription. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.

## Subscribing to Premium

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Today!**
3. Complete payment through Paddle (our payment provider)

Premium includes:

- Image attachments
- Custom domains
- Email subscribers
- Reply by email
- Analytics
- Avatar
- Custom CSS
- Human customer support

## Viewing your subscription

The Billing page shows:

- Your current plan
- Next payment date
- Payment amount

## Updating your card

If you need to update your payment details:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Update Card Details**
3. Enter your new card information

## Cancelling your subscription

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm on the next page

After cancelling:

- You keep Premium features until your current billing period ends
- Your account then reverts to the free tier
- Your posts and pages remain, but you lose access to premium features

## Restarting a cancelled subscription

Changed your mind? Before your subscription expires, you can restart it from the Billing page by clicking **Restart your subscription**.
